====================
INFORMATION CATEGORIES TO EXTRACT
====================

### 1. TENDER IDENTIFICATION (CPPP)
- **Tender ID**: "NIT No." format (e.g., "NIT No. 2024/ABC/123")
- Tender title/Project name
- Department/Ministry
- Issuing authority/office
- Location (State, City)
- Funding source (if any)
- Organization address, email, telephone
- **WARNING**: Tender ID is NEVER a date like "01-01-2024"

### 2. IMPORTANT DATES & TIMELINE (CPPP SPECIFIC)
**CRITICAL - Look for exact labels:**
- **"Date & time of issue"**: Extract EXACTLY as labeled
- **"Due Date & time of Submission"**: Extract EXACTLY as labeled
- Tender publication date
- Bid start date/time
- Bid end date/time
- Pre-bid meeting date and location
- Technical bid opening date/time
- Financial bid opening date/time
- Expected award date
- Contract start date
- **IMPORTANT**: Dates must include time (HH:MM:SS) if provided

### 3. PROJECT SCOPE & DESCRIPTION
- What is being procured (goods, services, works)
- Detailed specifications
- Quantity and units
- Location/Delivery location
- Timeline and duration
- Key deliverables

### 4. ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS (CPPP SPECIFIC)
**CRITICAL: EXTRACT COMPLETE ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA**

Look in Section 6.2 or "Qualification Requirements" for:

**1. EXPERIENCE CRITERIA (May have multi-call structure):**
- **1st Call**: Experience requirement for first call
- **2nd Call**: Experience requirement for second call
- **3rd Call**: Experience requirement for third call
- Similar works/projects definition
- Completion timeline (e.g., "completed in last 5 years")
- Work value requirements (if specified as percentage of contract value)
- Extract EXACTLY as written - don't summarize

**2. FINANCIAL ELIGIBILITY:**
- Minimum average annual turnover (3-year average)
- Solvency certificate requirements
- Net worth requirements
- Bank guarantee/financial security mandates

**3. TECHNICAL INFRASTRUCTURE (CPPP Specific):**
- Computer System requirements: Specs, OS, processor
- Broadband/Internet requirements: Speed (Mbps), type
- DSC (Digital Signature Certificate): Type, validity
- Other technical requirements

**4. LICENSES & REGISTRATIONS:**
- Trade license
- GST registration
- PAN
- Professional tax registration
- Contractor licenses (Electrical, etc.)
- Industry certifications
- ISO certifications

**5. LEGAL & OTHER ELIGIBILITY:**
- Non-blacklisting affidavit/declaration
- Validity certificates
- Consortium/JV allowed (Yes/No)
- International bidders allowed (Yes/No)
- MSME/Startup exemptions

### 5. FINANCIAL REQUIREMENTS
- EMD/Bid security (amount, exemptions)
- Tender document fee
- Performance security/bank guarantee
- Retention money
- Payment terms and schedule
- Advance payment provisions
- Mobilization advance
- EMD refund conditions

### 6. DOCUMENTS REQUIRED (CPPP ENVELOPE STRUCTURE)
**CRITICAL - CPPP has explicit Online vs Offline separation:**

**1. OFFLINE SUBMISSION DOCUMENTS (Physical/Hardcopy):**
Look for section "Offline Submission" or hardcopy requirements:
- Original tender fee (DD/Cheque)
- Original EMD
- Power of Attorney
- Physical documents with original signatures
- Any documents explicitly marked "Original only"
- Physical submission address and instructions

**2. ONLINE SUBMISSION DOCUMENTS (Scanned/PDF):**
Look for Envelope structures (Envelope-1, Envelope-2, etc.):
- **Envelope-1 (Technical Bid)**: Technical documents
  - Annual average turnover certificate
  - Experience certificates
  - Similar work certificates
  - Technical capability documents
  - ISO certificates
  - Undertakings and declarations
- **Envelope-2 (Financial Bid)**: Financial documents/Price bid
  - Price schedule/Bill of Quantities
  - Technical specifications compliance
  - Financial proposal
- **Envelope-3** (if exists): Additional documents

**EXACT EXTRACTION RULE:**
Extract document names EXACTLY as they appear in envelope tables. Example row format:
"Annual Average Turnover | Experience Certificates | Technical Capability | ISO Certificate"

**3. MASTER DOCUMENTS LIST:**
Combine all required documents in `documents_required` array.

### 7. ELIGIBILITY SUMMARY (SPECIAL CPPP CLAUSES)
- **Right to Reject without Assigning Reason**: Explicitly search for this CPPP-specific clause
- **Right to Split the Tender/Work**: Explicitly search for this CPPP-specific clause
- These are often in Section 4.6 or similar

### 8. TERMS & CONDITIONS
- Delivery terms
- Warranty/guarantee period
- Liquidated damages formula (e.g., "2% per week up to 10%")
- Termination conditions
- Force majeure
- Arbitration/Dispute resolution
- Insurance requirements
- Blacklisting conditions

### 9. EVALUATION & SELECTION
- Evaluation method (single-stage, two-stage)
- Evaluation criteria and weightage
- Technical evaluation process
- Financial evaluation process
- Selection method (L1, QCBS, QBS, etc.)
- Pass/Fail criteria

### 10. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION
- Contact details for queries
- Query submission process
- Special conditions
- Compliance requirements
- Anti-corruption/Integrity clauses

### 11. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Provide 3-4 sentences: What's being procured, where, critical eligibility (especially multi-call if applicable), key dates, and financial commitments.
